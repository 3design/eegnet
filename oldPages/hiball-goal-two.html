HIBALL

Virtual BigBrain and Simulation Platform

HIBALL will generate a microscopic 3D map of functional neuroanatomy at the cellular level and extend it with data from other modalities, including neurotransmitter-receptor distributions and high-resolution structural connectivity from polarized light imaging (PLI). Tools to visualize and manipulate such high resolution data will be developed in parallel.

A community in the making.

For information please email us at info@bigbrainproject.org
